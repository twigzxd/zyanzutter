Why Us

Traditional service is less efficient

Traditional maid service require customer take leadership and assign tasks. The performance is fluctuate base on staff personal reason. Due to lack of professional equipment and personal knowledge limitation for chemicals, they make have chemical over use or un diluted chemicals that threat customers healthy.

Zutter

We are highly organized to maximize work efficiency. Our pricing is project based, which eliminate the concern for customer to pay our staff for breaking time. We equip with most advanced equipment and chemicals in the industry to cleaning space based on its feature and we are able to handle all types of situation and scale of cleaning task. We not only killed the bacteria and cleaned your space, but also provide a good chemical balance for you and your children.

Traditional service lack of professional training

Zutter

Zutter

Zutter has its own professional team for training. We have two layers of serivces: basic cleaning and biological environment balance.
